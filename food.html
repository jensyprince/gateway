Mangu
Mangu (mashed plantain) is a very well known and favorite dominican dish. The most popular way to eat mangu is with los tres golpes which are fried cheese, fried salami and fried eggs. In the domiinican republic you can get a full plate for only $5.85.
Sancocho
Sancocho is a meat and root stew that is always made in very large quantities usually for a party or a special occasion. It is very special dish because of its long preparation time and its many ingredients. In the Dominican Republic if you'd like to buy a bowl of sancocho it would cost you around $7.00
Dominican Chimi
The Dominican chimichurri (chimi) is a very popular street food sold just about anywhere. A chimi consists of a Dominican bread known as pan de agua, shredded cabbage, meat and a special sauce. The chimi is usually never made at home so the recepies vary from the street vendors you buy these famous burgers from. The price of a chimi is around $1-$2 dollars.
Habichuela con dulce
Habichuela con dulce might be one of the most unique desserts you caan try. Habichuela con dulce is made from red beans different spices such as cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg, and habichuela con dulce is usually eaten with milk cookies. The price for habichuela con dulce is around $0.50 but habichuela con dulce is usually shared with neighbors and friends and is made for holidays and special occasions.

Xtreme-Buggy
Enjoy buggying around the Punta Cana scenery as you enjoy a full day exploring the beaches and Dominican country side. It is an exciting activity to do no matter the age. The average cost for a full day is around $300 and the experiance makes it worth it.
Snorkeling
Snorkeling in catalina island is a full day all inclusive tour for only $80 if you're and adult and kids under six years old get to enjoy snorkeling for free. It includes a Dominican style BBQ prepared by a renowned chef.
Horseback Riding
Experience a 6 hour long all inclusive excursion that includes Horseback riding, Zip Lining and a tour around the ranch where you will get to sample localy grown Vanilla and Cocoa. Its an amazing activity to do with the whole family and it would only cost $99 per person.
Waterfall Exploration
If you love exploring and adventure this is perfect for you. You will get to discover 27 different waterfalls in the Damajagua river. children 8+ are allowed to come along for this experience with an adult for $79 per person.
Monkey Land
The Monkey Land adventure in Puerto Plata lets you interact with native squirrel monkeys and learn about their natural habitat and behaviors. This experience is perfect for all age groups. You enjoy 4.5 hours with the wildlife for $90 per person.
Santo Domingo Tour
The Santo Domingo city tour will immerse you into the cultural and historicle heritage of Santo Domingo you will get to visit the Colonial zone and see a lot of historical buildings and monuments. With lunch provided. This full day tour is perfect for Everyone no matter the age and it costs $88.
